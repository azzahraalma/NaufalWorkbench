Drone Quadcopter

A drone quadcopter is a UAV with four propellers used for aerial tasks. It features a lightweight main frame, propellers and brushless motors for lift and control, landing gear, a camera module, internal electronics (GPS, sensors), and mounting screws for assembly.

Rollator

A rollator is a wheeled mobility aid for elderly users. It has a metal frame, ergonomic handle grips with brakes, four rubber wheels, a central seat, a storage basket, a folding support bracket for compact storage, and fasteners to hold all parts securely.

Remote Control

A remote control is a wireless device for operating electronics using IR or RF signals. It includes a plastic casing, rubber keypad, internal PCB (circuit board), AA batteries, battery cover, IR LED emitter, and screws to hold the parts together.

Roller Conveyor

A roller conveyor is a mechanical system used to transport goods in logistics or manufacturing. It consists of a main frame (steel or aluminum), rollers (steel or PVC) for moving items, roller brackets for support, fasteners to assemble components, and directional arrow labels to indicate flow.
